About Me

I recently completed my PhD in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Northeastern University, Boston, MA. Under the supervison of Prof. Stratis Ioannidis and Prof. Dana Brooks, my doctoral research focused on "Permutation Invariant Graph Learning".
My research interests lie in the fields of machine learning, graph mining, and optimization. I'm passionate about developing innovative algorithms and models that can improve the efficiency and effectiveness of real-world systems.
During my PhD studies, I designed and implemented five impactful projects utilizing Python, PyTorch, and TensorFlow.
